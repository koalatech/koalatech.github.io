About me

Networks, Homelabs & Smart Homes

I’m Richard M. Pabelona Jr. — a networking technology enthusiast, homelab runner, and an associate professor passionate about tinkering with things, making sure those things work, and sharing that knowledge in the classroom and beyond.

My work sits at the intersection of hands-on networking, virtualization, and applied research. Whether I’m routing packets, spinning up VMs, or writing lecture materials, I’m always looking for the elegant solution underneath the complexity.

I run a homelab that mirrors real-world enterprise topologies — a testbed for learning, experimentation, and fun. My networking stack includes MikroTik, Cisco switches, and TP-Link gear, with simulation via Packet Tracer and GNS3.

I’m also deep into Smart Home technology — I have an existing smart home setup with various smart appliances and lighting, running Home Assistant on an LXC container, Google Home, Tuya / Smart Life, and Samsung SmartThings. I write setup guides covering all of these platforms.

My Stack

Homelab & Smart Home Stack

Networking gear, virtualization, and smart home platforms running in my home infrastructure.

  • PVE

    Proxmox VE

    Open-source hypervisor platform running KVM virtual machines and LXC containers. The backbone of my homelab compute cluster.

    Hypervisor KVM LXC
  • π

    Pi-hole

    Network-wide DNS sinkhole for ad and tracker blocking. Provides visibility into DNS queries across all devices on the network.

    DNS Ad-blocking Security
  • MT

    MikroTik CHR

    Cloud Hosted Router running RouterOS in a VM — used for advanced routing labs, MPLS experimentation, and firewall rule testing.

    RouterOS BGP MPLS
  • AL

    Alpine Linux

    Minimal, security-oriented Linux distribution used for lightweight containers and service VMs where footprint and attack surface matter.

    Linux Minimal Security
  • VM

    VMware Workstation

    Desktop hypervisor for rapid prototyping and pre-production testing of lab topologies before deploying them on the Proxmox cluster.

    VMware Desktop Prototyping
  • HA

    Home Assistant LXC

    Open-source home automation platform deployed as an LXC container on Proxmox. Central hub for all smart home devices, automations, and dashboards.

    Smart Home LXC Automation
  • 🏠

    Smart Home Platforms

    Google Home, Tuya, Smart Life, and Samsung SmartThings integrated throughout the home — controlling smart appliances, lighting, and other IoT devices.

    Google Home Tuya Smart Life SmartThings
  • CS

    Cisco Switches

    Cisco Catalyst switches for layer-2/3 enterprise switching labs. VLANs, STP, inter-VLAN routing, and access-list configurations on actual hardware.

    Cisco IOS VLAN STP
  • TPL

    TP-Link Gear

    TP-Link access points and switches for Wi-Fi coverage and home network segmentation, complementing the MikroTik routing layer.

    Wi-Fi Access Points Switching
  • SIM

    Packet Tracer & GNS3

    Cisco Packet Tracer for teaching and classroom simulations; GNS3 for advanced multi-vendor topologies mirroring real production environments.

    Packet Tracer GNS3 Simulation
